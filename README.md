## What BTC War Does

BTC War is designed for people who want a faster, more visual way to follow Bitcoin market activity right in the browser. Instead of reading through flat tables or separate charts, it combines live BTC pricing, order flow, market depth, and executed trades into a single scene that is easier to interpret at a glance.

At the center of the app is a 3D battlefield rendered with web technologies including WebGL and Three.js. That makes it a practical fit for traders, analysts, and visualization fans who want to track buy and sell pressure, liquidity clusters, and trade execution behavior in one interactive view.

Launch the app in your browser to begin monitoring live BTC market activity.

1. Load the page.
2. Review the current BTC price.

5. Follow executed trades inside the 3D scene.
